E4 Elaborate — Short Circuits, Fuses & Circuit Breakers ELABORATE
⚠ Short Circuit — A short circuit happens when the LIVE wire touches the NEUTRAL directly (near-zero resistance). The current becomes dangerously high, heating wires until fire starts.
Short Circuit MCQ — In a short circuit at home, what immediately stops the danger? 1 pt

🔴 Fuse

• Contains a thin wire that melts when current exceeds its rated value
• Connected in SERIES in the circuit
• Single use — must be replaced after blowing
• Ratings: 1A · 2A · 5A · 10A · 13A · 16A

🔵 Circuit Breaker

• Uses a bimetallic strip or magnetic trip that opens the circuit
• Connected in SERIES in the circuit
• Reusable — just press RESET after tripping
• Same current ratings as fuses
