Key Formula
v = f × λ
v = speed of light = 3 × 10⁸ m/s
f = frequency (Hz) · λ = wavelength (m)
Higher frequency → shorter wavelength → more energy
Lower frequency → longer wavelength → less energy
The Golden Rule
All EM waves:
✓ Are transverse waves
✓ Travel at 3×10⁸ m/s in vacuum
✓ Need no medium to travel
✓ Transfer energy without transferring matter
✓ Are caused by oscillating electric & magnetic fields
