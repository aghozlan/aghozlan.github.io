📚 Key Words & Reference Sheet
Enzyme: biological catalyst that speeds up a specific chemical reaction
Specificity: an enzyme only works on one type of substrate
Substrate: the molecule an enzyme acts upon
Diffusion: movement of a substance from high concentration to low concentration
Alveolus (pl. alveoli): tiny air sacs in the lungs where gas exchange occurs
Amino acids → Proteins: building blocks join to form proteins (synthesis)
Chromosome: structure in the cell nucleus that carries genetic information
Somatic cell: any body cell (not a reproductive cell)
Human somatic cells: 46 chromosomes = 23 pairs
Male formula: 44 + XY (46, XY)
Female formula: 44 + XX (46, XX)
Protein synthesis: amino acids link together inside cells
