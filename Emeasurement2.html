E2 Circuit Diagrams — Ammeter & Voltmeter Symbols EXPLORE ▾

Now we convert the real multimeter into circuit diagram symbols. The small black dot next to each symbol represents the COM probe connection point.

Ammeter (A) — series Voltmeter (V) — parallel COM point (●)
🔵 Ammeter in Series: Current passes through the ammeter — must be in the main wire.

🟡 Voltmeter in Parallel: Connects to both sides of the component being measured.

E3 Readings & Rules — When Things Go Wrong EXPLAIN ▾

What does the meter tell you?

Ammeter reads 0 A
→ Circuit is open. No current can flow.
Voltmeter reads battery voltage
→ Measured component is open/broken.
Voltmeter reads 0 V
→ Both probes are on points at the same potential — no voltage difference to measure.
Ammeter reads very high
→ Possible short circuit.
