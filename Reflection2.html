2 Image Formation in a Plane Mirror

Move the candle left and right (in front of the center mirror). What do you observe about the image?

Obj. Distance (do) = 150
Img. Distance (di) = 150

💡 Conclusion: The image is always Virtual, Upright, and at the same distance (do = di)!

Practical Challenge: Draw and Capture! 📸

Now it's your turn for practical application. Get a white paper, pencil, and a ruler, and draw the following clearly and neatly:

  • Draw the surface of a plane mirror (with short lines on the back/opaque side).
  • Draw the Normal line as a dashed line perpendicular to the mirror.
  • Draw an Incident Ray and label it (I), and a Reflected Ray and label it (R).
  • Clearly indicate the angle of incidence and the angle of reflection.
  • Write your full name and section by hand on the same paper.
