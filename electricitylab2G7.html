E2 · Explore

Open vs Closed Circuits

Tap the switch buttons below to see what happens to the circuit — and understand why position of the switch matters for current flow.

🔓 Open Circuit
The switch creates a gap in the circuit.
Current cannot flow — the path is broken.
The lamp stays OFF. Energy cannot reach the receiver.
🔒 Closed Circuit
The switch completes the circuit loop.
Current flows freely — the path is complete.
The lamp lights ON. Energy reaches the receiver.
