// About
"In Lebanon's classrooms, technology is not a luxury — it is the bridge between crisis and continuity."
Ahmad Ghozlan is a physics and science teacher based in Lebanon, developing AI-powered interactive lessons that bring the 5E inquiry model to life in online and hybrid classrooms.

Each module combines live simulations, misconception-targeted exercises, AI-assisted feedback, and automated grade collection — building toward a research-based portfolio in educational technology.
