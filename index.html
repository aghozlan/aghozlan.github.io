About This Library
Built for Lebanese Classrooms

Every lab here is designed around the 5E inquiry model — Engage, Explore, Explain, Elaborate, Evaluate. Students interact with live canvas simulations, receive Socratic AI feedback via Groq, and submit results directly to Ahmad's Google Sheets grade book through Apps Script.

No app to install. No account to create. One link, any device, any network condition.
